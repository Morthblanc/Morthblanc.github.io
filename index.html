01 // CORE EXPERTISE

What I work on

⚗️

Hydrometallurgy

Leaching, precipitation, crystallisation, and selective separation of rare earth elements from primary ores and secondary waste streams.

🧪

Solvent Extraction

D2EHPA based extraction and stripping, with focus on rare earth and iron separation and pH behaviour in chloride and sulfate media.

🧲

Magnet & Battery Recycling

Flexible aqueous routes for NdFeB permanent magnet waste, plus recycling of lithium iron phosphate (LFP) and NMC battery black mass into high purity products.

🏭

Pilot Scale Operations

Moving processes from laboratory to pilot, including continuous acid leaching of REE rich slag and flowsheet validation.

💻

Data & Modeling

Python for process data analysis and automation, mass and energy balance models for scale up decisions.

🌍

Critical Raw Materials

Recovery of REE, thorium, barite, fluorite, rubidium, and niobium from complex ores within EU and national programs.

03 // RESEARCH & INNOVATION

Selected works

2024 – 2027 · EU HORIZON EUROPE · HARMONY

Aqueous Recovery of Rare Earths from NdFeB Magnet Waste

Doctoral research within the HARMONY project, a holistic approach to enhance the recyclability of rare earth permanent magnets from any waste stream. The work covers selective leaching, iron management, and D2EHPA solvent extraction, including an investigation into pH measurement instability in chloride media driven by the absence of a sulfate buffer, multiple iron chloride species, and HCl co extraction by the extractant.

Pilot Scale Continuous Leaching of REE Rich Slag

A continuous leaching campaign scaling REE recovery from rich slag toward industrial relevance, processing around 45 kg of feed and recovering roughly 10 kg of mixed REE oxalate product.
